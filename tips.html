Many goals have notes or commentary attached to them. Curious what they all are? Enable Kibitzing and view a board with every goal on it. (This board won't work in-game, it's purely for Viewer illustration.)

[Base Expedition and v1.08+] Vista point locations can be found on these community maps from Regions - Official Rain World Wiki:

  • Full Downpour World Map
  • Looks To The Moon (DM)
  • Metropolis (LC)
  • Silent Construct (CL)
  • The Rot (RM)
  • Undergrowth (UG)

[v0.9 to v1.07] Courtesy Bronsej, these include customized Vista Point locations:

  • Full Downpour World Map (all regions, non-timeline-specific versions)
  • Looks To The Moon (DM)
  • Metropolis (LC)
  • Silent Construct (CL)
  • The Rot (RM)
  • Undergrowth (UG)
  • Waterfront Facility (LM)

(As of v1.08, the customized vista points are disabled by default; they are planned to become available as an option in a future version. If you liked them, they can always be entered manually; see the bv.maps.vistas or bv.enums.vista_code arrays for suggested rooms and coordinates.)
